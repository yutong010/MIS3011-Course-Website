Advanced AI for Business: From Theories to Applications

MIS3011 | CUHK(SZ)

Course Overview

Advanced AI for Business: From Theories to Applications is designed for those ready to explore the cutting-edge technology of artificial intelligence from a business perspective. The course spans 15 intricately designed weeks that merge theory with hands-on experience, unraveling AI's constituents, demonstrating the role of data and algorithms, diving into the world of machine learning and deep learning, and eventually exploring the ethics and regulations controlling AI. The course encapsulates real-world case studies, lab sessions, group activities, and many more engaging tools that ensure a comprehensive understanding of how AI can transform the business landscape.

Learning Outcomes

This course focuses on three core skill pillars that prepare you for AI applications in business:

Skill Pillar 1
Predicting the Future with Data
  • Master Logistic Regression for business decisions
  • Deep dive into Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  • Build a customer churn prediction model for a bank
  • Act as a data scientist to predict personal loan offer acceptance
Skill Pillar 2
Teaching Computers to See
  • Learn about powerful Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
  • Master the efficient technique of Transfer Learning
  • Build a "Hot Dog/Not Hot Dog" image classifier from scratch
  • Develop a touchless hand navigation control system using MediaPipe
Skill Pillar 3
Mastering the Language of AI
  • Learn essential techniques for Natural Language Processing
  • Use Hugging Face library for sentiment analysis
  • Look inside the black box of LLMs
  • Explore Transformers to GenAI Project Lifecycle

MIS3011 (Broad Focus): Become an "AI Generalist"
This course offers a systematic overview of classic AI technologies from the last decade, including neural networks, deep learning, and computer vision. Approximately 25-30% of the course is dedicated to Generative AI. It is ideal for students who want a comprehensive understanding of modern AI.

MIS3012 (Deep Focus): Become an "AI Specialist"
This course concentrates on the latest Generative AI technologies, such as Large Language Models (LLM) and AI Agent application development. It is designed for students who wish to dive deep into Generative AI and build hands-on projects.
